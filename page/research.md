I am interested in *meta-learning*, *domain adaptation*, and *black-box optimization*, heading toward *lifelong and open-world machine learning systems* with computer-vision orientation. In particular, I am aiming to develop computer vision models that can be continuously optimized with minimal training data or less human supervision and effectively generalize into other domains outside of training distribution.
